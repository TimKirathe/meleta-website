Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 07/06/2025

Meleta is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

  • Email address – Used for analytics and is linked to your identity.
  • User ID – Used for analytics and is linked to your identity.
  • Product interaction data – Used for analytics and is linked to your identity.

How We Use Your Information

We use this information to understand how users interact with Meleta, improve app performance, and enhance the user experience.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data is used internally and may be shared with trusted service providers for analytics purposes.

Your Rights

Upon deleting your account with us, the data we store is also deleted - we do not retain it for any period of time. Whilst you have an account with us, you have the right to request access to, correct, or delete your data. Please contact us at support@meleta.org.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated. Significant changes will be communicated through the app or this website.

If you have questions, contact us at support@meleta.org
